Pride or Coop?

The LGBTQ+ Pride Flag

Has six colors.

They are red, orange, yellow, green, blue and purple.

Does not have light blue.

Colors are not vibrant.

The Cooperativism Movement flag

Has seven colors.

They are red, orange, yellow, green, light blue, dark blue and violet.

Has light blue (also sometimes bright purple).

Colors are vibrant.
